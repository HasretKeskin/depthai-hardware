Overview
********

**OAK-1 W** features a **Wide FOV** color sensor (:ref:`IMX378`) on a tiny, yet performant, device.

OAK-1 W isn’t a standard USB camera. It’s a 4-trillion-operations-per-second AI powerhouse that performs your AI 
models on-board, so that your host is free to do whatever you need it to do.
